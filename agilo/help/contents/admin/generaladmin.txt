= General Admin Panel =
[[PageOutline]]

Located in [/admin/agilo/general Admin/Agilo/General]

In this panel you are allowed to enable or disable the Agilo UI, and define if you want the time calculation to be shown in [agilo-help:scrum/IdealHour Ideal Days] or you want them to be in [agilo-help:scrum/IdealHour#idealdays Ideal Hours].
